Privacy Policy

Yarn Stash is an offline-first app. All data you enter is stored locally on your device in the app's Application Support directory and is never transmitted to us or any third party. We do not collect, sell, or share any personal data.

The only network communication in this app is Apple's own StoreKit purchase and restore flow, handled entirely by Apple, for unlocking the Pro tier. We never see your payment details.

If you delete the app, all locally stored data is removed with it.
